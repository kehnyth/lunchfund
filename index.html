Our Mission & Impact

‚ö†Ô∏è The Challenge

Hunger remains the world's number one health risk, claiming more lives annually than AIDS, malaria, and tuberculosis combined. The connection between hunger and poverty creates a devastating cycle that traps families for generations.

Globally, 66 million primary school-age children attend classes hungry every day, with 23 million of these children in Africa alone. These children face an impossible choice: skip school to search for food or attend classes while battling debilitating hunger that destroys their ability to learn and grow.

‚ú® Our Solutions

  • Nutritious School Meals: For just $0.30 per meal, we provide children with the nutrition they need to focus, learn, and build a foundation for escaping poverty through education.
  • Blockchain Transparency: We leverage cryptocurrency and smart contracts to ensure every donation is tracked, transparent, and directly reaches beneficiaries while empowering families with digital financial tools.
  • Scalable Impact: Starting with a pilot program at the Jolly-Mercy Learning Centre in Uganda, we've successfully expanded to feed over 6,000 students and teachers across seven African schools.

Binance Charity's Lunch for Children Program has successfully expanded to 10 additional schools in Uganda, demonstrating the transformative power of combining nutrition with education to break the poverty cycle.
